Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the I Ching Application ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience using our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

The App does not collect or store any personally identifiable information.

2.2 User-Generated Content

We do not store the questions you submit for I Ching readings.

2.3 Technical Information

We collect limited technical information to help maintain and improve the App's functionality. This includes:

- Crash logs

- Error logs

- Screen analytics

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be linked to individual users.

3. How We Use Information

The technical information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

- To maintain and improve the App's performance

- To identify and fix technical issues

- To analyze user interaction with the App's interface for improvement purposes

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API to generate personalized I Ching readings. The questions you submit are processed through this API but are not stored by us or OpenAI.

4.2 Firebase

We use Firebase to collect crash and log information. This helps us identify and resolve technical issues quickly.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. The technical information we collect may be shared with our service providers (OpenAI and Firebase) solely for the purpose of operating and improving the App.

6. Data Retention

As we do not collect personal information or store user questions, there is no personal data to retain. Technical information is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy.

7. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data for you to access, correct, or delete. However, you can stop all collection of information by the App by uninstalling it.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@fernandodev.com.
